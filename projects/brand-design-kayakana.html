Kayakana Skincare

Brand Identity Design | Website UI UX Design

01. Introduction

Created to celebrate skin’s natural vitality, Kayakana formulates high-performance products using safe, effective ingredients inspired by nature and validated by scientific research. The brand embodies clarity, purity, and innovation in modern skincare.


Task Given: To reposition, redefine and create a fresh new look for the brand. Starting from the logo type, colors, typeface and packaging design. From the creation of the website and social media posts.

02. Research and Challenges

Establishing a cohesive brand identity from the ground up posed a challenge. I had to create a visual language—logo, packaging, typography, color system, and digital presence—that felt unified, recognizable, and aligned with Kayakana’s positioning of “clean beauty meets nature + science.”
